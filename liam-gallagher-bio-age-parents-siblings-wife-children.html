Liam Gallagher is an English singer and songwriter.

Liam Gallagher achieved fame as the lead vocalist of the rock band Oasis from 1991 to 2009.

Liam Gallagher later fronted the rock band Beady Eye from 2009 to 2014 before starting a successful solo career in 2017.
