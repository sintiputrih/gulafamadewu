Little Mix Release Preview Of 'Power' Music Video (Featuring Stormzy)

 Posted on August 18, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  326 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Little Mix – made up of members Jesy Nelson, Jade Thirlwall, Leigh-Anne Pinnock and Perrie Edwards – have delighted their fans (better known as ‘Mixers’) by releasing a preview of their new music video for recent single ‘Power’ (featuring Stormzy).

The video is set to be releasing at midnight BST TONIGHT – and we can’t wait to see the video for the incredible girl power anthem! This video certainly seems different to the other videos that the girls have done over the years, with the video even set to feature their Mums – the ‘Mummy Mixers!’
