Actress Totally Comfortable Being Directed by Her Grandfather While Naked

 Posted on February 4, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  93 words  |  Martina Birk

Georgia Warner is a 21-year-old actress co-starring in the new East Hampton–based, Alec Baldwin–headlined production of boy-who-loves-horses-too-much psychodrama,Equus. The role requires nudity, a fact that “hardly registers” for Warner, because her step-grandfather, Oscar-winning costume and set designer Tony Walton, is the play’s director. Warner says having her “popup” direct her in her birthday suit is “very comfortable for me. I know he’s looking out for my best interests.” What, talking to your grandfather while naked might make you uncomfortable? Your bad. [Gatecrasher/NYDN]
