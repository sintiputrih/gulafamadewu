Alexandra Limon Biography

Alexandra Limon joined our Washington, DC, newsroom in August 2017.  She covers our state Congressional delegation and the federal government stories that affect us here at home.

Alexandra Limon Age

She was born on 03/17/1976. As of 2019, she is 43 years old. California girl, who always wanted to live on the east coast and am so happy to be at FOX 5 and living in the nation’s capital!

Alexandra Limon Husband/Kid

She is a married woman though she has not revealed the name of her husband. Alex and her husband are the proud parents of a son named Lincoln, who was born in DC. They love spending their spare time hiking, swimming, traveling and eating food from around the world, as well as spending time with their dog Cooper and cats Sam and Midori.

Alexandra Limon Education

Alex grew up in sunny southern California and graduated from California State University, Fullerton, with a degree in Communications.

Alexandra Limon Career

Alex began covering local news in Washington, DC, in 2014.  Before DC, she worked as a news reporter all around the west, reporting in both English and Spanish, at TV stations in Reno, Fresno, Seattle, and Los Angeles.  She reported for the Telemundo network from the US-Mexico border in Arizona on issues like immigration and drug trafficking.

Before joining FOX 5 in June 2014, I worked at FOX 11 in Los Angeles.  While working in the second largest news market in the country, I covered major stories including the shooting that shut down LAX.  I also provided around the clock coverage for several days from San Francisco, on the deadly crash of Asiana flight 214.
