Hailey Bieber Takes On Paris in Underwear, Sheer Tights, and No Pants

 Posted on March 18, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  329 words  |  Larita Shotwell

The pantless trend has officially made waves over at Paris Fashion Week.

Hailey Bieber ushered in the risqué fad with her latest look, which took a cue from a similar outfit worn last year by best friend and fellow supermodel Kendall Jenner.

Bieber ventured out into the City of Lights on Monday evening. Her ensemble consisted of a clingy taupe turtleneck from the Row tucked into a pair of black high-rise hot shorts by Petar Petrov and sheer black tights from celeb-favored brand Calzedonia. She kept warm with a long black leather trench coat with a hemline that nearly swept the floor. To accessorize, the Rhode founder added a black belt with a rectangular gold buckle, inky black pumps, a black Jil Sander purse, narrow black oval sunglasses, and sculptural gold earrings.

Splash News

Last November, Jenner wore a similar outfit in Los Angeles that also relied on her own black underwear and sheer Calzedonia tights. At the time, the 818 founder finished off the monochromatic look with a slouchy black knit sweater layered over a white long-sleeved shirt, black satin slingback pumps, black rectangular shades, and a woven black Bottega Veneta bag with a glossy gold handle.

In an interview for Bazaar’s September 2022 cover story, Bieber talked about what it’s like to develop her own sense of style while living in the public eye. “I get photographed so much that I feel like sometimes I put pressure on myself,” she said at the time. “Even if I’m just throwing on jeans and a T-shirt, I want it to be a dope pair of jeans and a great T-shirt!”
