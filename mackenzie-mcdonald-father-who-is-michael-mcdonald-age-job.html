Michael McDonald Job

Michael McDonald is an oral surgeon at McDonald Oral Surgery. He performs oral & maxillofacial surgery procedures of wisdom teeth, dental implants, anesthesia, bone grafting, oral pathology, facial trauma, and Temporo Mandibular Joint (TMJ) Treatment.

He is a Diplomate of the National Dental Board of Anesthesia and the American Board of Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery. Michael has been practicing oral and maxillofacial surgery for more than twenty years. He graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Biology from the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA), and then moved on to the University of Pennsylvania, School of Dental Medicine, where he earned his Doctor of Dental Medicine degree.

The University of California at San Francisco (UCSF) is where he finished both his general practice residency and his training in oral and maxillofacial surgery. In Pinole, California, Michael runs a full-service oral and maxillofacial surgery practice.

Michael is also active in social responsibility. He is adamant about giving back to the neighborhood where he lives and works. Michael and colleagues provide assistance to the following organizations through cash and in-kind gifts, board member representation, mentorship, and advocacy support.

His CSR works extend to Asian Health Services, Bay Area Hispano Institute for Advancement (BAHIA, Inc.), Contra Costa Dental Society/Lebow Foundation, Court Appointed Special Advocates (CASA) of Alameda County, Students Rising Above, and UC Berkeley – Social Welfare, Student Parent Center, Athletics.
