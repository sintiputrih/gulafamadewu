See Inside the Kardashians' Lavish 2023 Easter Celebrations

 Posted on January 19, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  403 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

This past Sunday, the Kardashian-Jenner clan gathered at Kris Jenner's La Quinta mansion to celebrate Easter—and they did so in the most luxe way possible.

The family shared snippets of their holiday on Instagram, including their personalized swag bags, the Easter-themed tablescapes, an egg painting station, and more.

On her Instagram Story, matriarch Kris posted a sweet snap of herself posing with all of her granddaughters. She also gave a glimpse at the decor: a Peter Rabbit-themed kids' table, floral centerpieces awash in dazzling pastel hues, and a large arrangement of life-sized rabbit dolls.

Khloé Kardashian shared snaps of her four-year-old daughter, True, looking Easter-ready in a baby pink Gucci dress, matching pink bunny ears, a bedazzled nameplate "True" necklace, and a cross pendant necklace. In another post, the mom of two shared photos of all the baby cousins gathered around an outdoor table to paint their own Easter eggs. On her Instagram Story, she snapped pictures of the buffet table, which was covered in goodies like chocolate Easter rabbits and eggs, bagels, cupcakes, cookies, and a charcuterie spread.

Meanwhile, Kourtney Kardashian took to her Instagram Story to document all the personal details from the day, including personalized swag bags for her and husband Travis Barker. She also shared a look at her Easter outfit: a baby pink maxi dress with a red floral pattern, plus a lacy bustier and a wispy ruffled blouse.

Kylie Jenner additionally shared her Easter ensemble on Instagram, in which she posed in a ribbed Jean Paul Gaultier maxi dress. The pastel green piece featured a halter neckline and a flowing pleated skirt. The makeup mogul later posted highlights from the holiday, too, which included pictures of Stormi and Aire's hand-painted gift bags, her one-year-old son clad in glittering blue bunny ears, and the children's individually embroidered Easter egg hunt baskets.

Kendall Jenner posted her Easter look, as well, which consisted of a vintage '90s-era Chloé slip dress in a silver hue and trimmed with delicate white lace.
