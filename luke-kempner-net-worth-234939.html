About

Comedian and impressionist who gained fame doing impressions of celebrities on the popular television series Murder in Successville. He brought to life stars like Tom Daley. He also appeared on Celebrity Big Brother's Bit On The Side and has starred in episodes of Drunk History and The Tracey Ullman Show. 

Before Fame

He first rocketed to fame in 2012 when his video Downstairs At Downton went viral. He would then go on to take his one man show The Only Way Is Downton to festivals internationally. 

Trivia

Outside of comedy, he is a pianist, puppeteer, and a classically trained singer. He has even starred in numerous musical theatre productions, including playing Marius in Les Miserables. 

Family Life

His father is actor/producer Mark Kempner and his mother's name is Anna. His sister, Suzanna, is also an actor. 

Associated With

In 2009, he made his screen acting debut in the film Fathers of Girls opposite Ray Winstone.
