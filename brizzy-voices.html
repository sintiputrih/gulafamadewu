Brizzy Voices is an American YouTuber and voice actress known for her YouTube channel. Brizzy Voices is known for her voice impressions of fictional characters, such as Harry Potter, Pokémon, and the Disney Princesses.

Early Life

Brizzy Voices was born Anna Stewart Brisbin, on September 13, 1991. She was raised in South Carolina and later moved to California to pursue a professional voice acting career.

Currently, she is 31 years old and her birth sign is Virgo. She has an older sister. Moreover, she attended the Tisch School of the Arts at New York University,

where she majored in drama and completed the Stonestreet Studios acting program. And, she studied at New York University and graduated with a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree.

Brisbin is good friends with fellow YouTubers Tessa Netting and Smosh. She often collaborates with them for her YouTube videos.

The YouTuber, who is a diehard fan of Harry Potter, can be often seen wearing a Deathly Hallows necklace. She has starred in a few short films including ‘Dreamstone’ and ‘Pixey’ as well.

Career

Brizzy Voices created her main channel ‘Brizzy Voices’ on May 31, 2012. Since then, she has been posting crazy yet appealing videos on it.

Her channel has everything from crazy voice impressions and voice challenges to character-inspired makeup tutorials, funny games, and challenges.

Some of the popular videos on this channel are ‘Voice ALL the Original Pokemon’, ‘Disney Princess Impressions’, and ‘YouTuber Impressions’. The first video is Brisbin’s most popular video.

Having over 24 million views to date, this video features Brisbin voicing all the Pokemon characters.

The other two videos, with over 8.4 million views and more than 3.3 million views, feature various impressions of Disney princesses and YouTubers respectively.

Brisbin has done more than 12 makeup and hair tutorials related to famous characters, such as Ariel from The Little Mermaid, Bellatrix Lestrange from Harry Potter, Effie Trinket from The Hunger Games, and Harley Quinn from DC.

In addition, she has done multiple covers of popular songs, including ‘After Ever After’ by Jon Cozart, ‘Belle’ from ‘Beauty and the Beast,’ ‘Do You Want to Build a Snowman’ from ‘Frozen’ and ‘Impressions’ from ‘Alice in Wonderland.’

She uploaded a photo booth challenge video with fellow YouTuber Catie Wayne in 2015.

Furthermore, her side channel, which was launched in August 2012, features vlogs concerning her routine day-to-day life. This channel, titled ‘BrizzyVlogs,’ is followed by thousands of people.

More

In 2015, she co-narrated the novel ‘A Tale of Two Besties’. She had a voice acting role in a 2016 web series adaptation of the popular video game Happy Wheels, in which she played Janet (the Effective Shopper).

Similarly, in 2017, her voiceover challenge series Voice It! debuted on Facebook Watch. With fellow YouTuber Tessa Netting, she is the co-host of the podcast Fantastic Geeks and Where to Find Them, which was launched in 2018.

Beginning in May 2020 she started to use her Instagram to promote crowdfunding bail for arrested individuals In Portland.

Therefore, she is known for Final Fantasy VII Remake (2020), Indivisible (2019), and River City Girls (2019). Moreover, she was shortlisted for the Artisan Award at the 2017 Summer in the City Awards but lost to Jamie Jo.
