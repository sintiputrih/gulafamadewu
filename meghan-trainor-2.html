Career

Meghan Trainor started her career as a songwriter and performer. She wrote her first original song titled Give Me a Chance when she was just 13 years old. After that, she records and produce her compositions with the help of a production house named Logic Studio and later worked independently in a home studio.

From the year 2009 to 2013, she has released three albums which she had written, produced, recorded, and performed. In the year 2009, she was enrolled in the Summer Performance Program at the Berklee College of Music and released her debut album titled Meghan Trainor.

In the year 2010, she released her debut song titled Take Care of Our Soldiers, and the very next year she released the acoustic albums titled I’ll Sing with You and Only 17. In the year 2012, she signed a deal with Big Yellow Dog Music and decided to pursue her career as a songwriter-for-hire.
