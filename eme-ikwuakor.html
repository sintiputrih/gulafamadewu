Eme Ikwuakor is an American actor who is best known for portraying roles such as Dwayne Turner in On My Block, Gorgon in Marvel’s Inhumans, and Gabe in Ink. He has also appeared in a variety of other TV shows and films such as Hawaii Five-0, Silicon Valley, Castle, NCIS: Los Angeles, How to Get Away with Murder, Victorious, Outlaw, Miles Fisher: Finish What We Started, Moonfall, ’51 Dons, Everything Before Us, Moonfall, Concussion, Not Safe for Work, and many more.
