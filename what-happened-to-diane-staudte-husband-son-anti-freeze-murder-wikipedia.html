What Happened To Diane Staudte Husband And Son? 

Back in 2012, Diane Stautde's husband Mark died at the age of 61. At the time, they ruled out his death as a natural cause. There was a small ring of blood around his mouth. 

With Mark's insurance payout, Diane alongside her four children moved to a different house. In September of the same year, their son Shaun died in a similar manner.
