About Paige DeSorbo

 Posted on February 27, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  372 words  |  Martina Birk

Possessing diverse talents, Paige DeSorbo, a well-known American actress, model, television personality, social media influencer, host, and entrepreneur from Loudonville, New York, gained popularity as a cast member of the Bravo reality show Summer House when she joined in 2019.

Biography & Profile Summary

Family and Background

Paige is the daughter of Gary DeSorbo (father), and Kimberly DeSorbo (mother). However, she hasn’t revealed much about her siblings.

Love Life with Craig Conover

Paige DeSorbo is in a happy relationship with her boyfriend, Craig Conover.

The pair met in 2018 when she was committed to a relationship, and they grew closer while working for Winter House in 2021. They married in the fall of 2021 and continue to be together.

Physical Attributes

Standing at 5 feet 6 inches, Paige maintains a slim figure with an approximate weight of 60 kg. Her body measurements are 33-23-33 inches. She has black hair and brown eyes, enhancing her charming appearance.

Career & Profession

Aside from her role on Summer House, Paige works as a Fashion Writer for Less at Betches Media and serves as an Executive Assistant to the Vice President of Unscripted Television at ABC Television Lincoln Square Productions. She has also held positions such as Fashion Intern at Bollare and TV host/Production Assistant at Arfpro studios. Paige made her TV debut as a Beauty Influencer in the series “Beauty Trends Live” and has appeared in shows like “Winter House,” “Access Hollywood,” and as Tracy in “Spare Me.”

Net Worth
