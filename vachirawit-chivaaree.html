Vachirawit Chivaaree aka Bright Vachirawit, is a Thai actor, singer, television host, model and entertainer. As of 2023, Vachirawit Chivaaree’s net worth is $3 million. He is the recipient of numerous accolades, including the LINE Thailand People’s Choice Awards (2020) and Thailand Actors Award in Japan (2021).
