Chloe East Biography, Height, Age, Childhood, Family Life and Achievements

Chloe East is an American actress who rose to fame through her appearances in films such as Ice Cream and Kevin (Probably) Saves the World. Before becoming famous for her acting roles, Chloé was a dancer and a model. Even though she is still young, she has many fans who always want to know more about her. Here’s everything you need to know about the actress.

Chloe Est Biography, Childhood & Age

The actress was born in San Clemente, California on February 16, 2001. The multi-talented actress was also raised alongside her two brothers in her birthplace.

Growing up, Chloe East enjoyed dancing and modeling. In fact, she trained as an extensive dancer in hip hop, contemporary dance, and ballet among other types of dance.

In many ways, she had a normal childhood, except that she spent most of her time learning to dance. Although not much is known about her educational background, she took acting classes even before her teenage years.

She started her acting career in 2013 when she had a role in True Blood. Her next job as an actress came in 2016 when she appeared as Jessica Darling in Jessica Darling ‘s It List, then Val Wishart in Liv and Maddie and Willow Green in Ice Cream. Her next job came in 2017 when she played Reese Cabrera in Kevin (Probably) Saves the World

East also has a YouTube channel which she started in 2010. So far, the channel has amassed nearly 300,000 followers and nearly 16 million total views. The actress uses the channel to share DIY videos and various other general lifestyle stuff. That said, it can’t be said that she’s a regular vlogger, as she doesn’t make videos as regularly as one would expect.

Looking at his social media engagements, East has a decent following. Before mid-2018, she had already had more than 309 thousand followers on Instagram while she had more than 13 thousand followers on Twitter.

Family life

Chloe was raised in a very close family. Her father and mother, Howard East and Aimée East respectively, were very supportive of her. She also has two brothers, Alex East and Austin East.

Even though her fans have always tried to know more about the family life of the young actress, there is not much to know as she is the only member of the family who is in the entertainment business and she does not seem give more details about the family.

Nevertheless, it is known that her parents did some acting when she was little and they had a family friend whose name was Jeff. He was the one who got her to become an actress and he recommended a manager for the young actress after trying several scenes with her.

That said, her first theatrical performance was in The Wizard of Oz, followed by an appearance when she attended a weekly musical theater class in the Lullabye League.

Achievements

Throughout her career as an actress, dancer and model, Chloe has recorded quite a few achievements so far.

In 2014, she emerged as the winner of the National Miss Starbound as a contemporary soloist in the National Starbound pageant. The same year, she won the Revolution San Diego in the same category as well as the Miss Spotlight 2014 during the Spotlight Dance Cup competition.

In 2013, at the Phil Fabry SpotlightCup Vegas dance, Chloe also emerged as the Miss National Spotlight for Jazz Soloist. Again, at the same year’s Larissa S YAGP International Finals NYC, she emerged as a YAGP NYC finalist as a contemporary dancer.

Thanks to her versatility, she had the opportunity to appear in several commercials including Get Up And Go Go Fur Real Friends commercial, Bella Ballerina Skechers and a Staples commercial in 2014.

Finally, when it comes to her modeling exploits, the actress has worked with a number of brands and stores in the past. She modeled for Zara Terez which has now become Terez, and she served as a cover model for the theater costume catalog, retail clothing catalog, and Prima Dance magazine. Besides that, Chloe worked as a spokesperson for Miss Behave Girls while working for Discount Dance between 2012 and 2015.
