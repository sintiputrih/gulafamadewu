Seyi Tinubus biography, net worth, fact, career, awards and life story

 Posted on May 24, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  987 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Seyi Tinubu is an award-winning entrepreneur. He is the CEO/Chairman of Loatsad Promomedia and the Founder of the Noella Foundation. Seyi Tinubu is the son of Lagos State Chieftain – Asiwaju Bola Ahmed Tinubu.

Seyi Tinubu Background

Seyi Tinubu was born on the 15th of October, 1985, to Asiwaju Bola Ahmed Tinubu (the 12th Governor of Lagos State), and his Wife Oluremi Tinubu, who is currently a Senator of Nigeria. He is the first child of his parents’ four children. Seyi Tinubu schooled and grew up in the United States.

Seyi attended Holmewood House School for his primary education. Afterwards, he went to Milton Abbey for his secondary education and A-levels. While at Milton, he participated in rugby, athletics, and field hockey.

In 2004, he became captain of Milton Abbey for the season. After high school, Seyi went on to Lincoln University, and the University of Maryland, Baltimore County, where he obtained a Bachelor of Arts (B.A.), from 2004 – 2008.

In 2008, Seyi started studying Law at the University of Buckingham and graduated with a Bachelor of Laws (LLB) in 2010. After graduation, Seyi went to the Nigeria Law School and was called to Bar in 2013. While in law school, Seyi worked for three months as a legal intern with Oando Plc.

Soon after, he obtained a master’s degree in corporate and commercial law from the University of Buckingham.

Seyi Tinubu is married to Layla Jade Tinubu, and they are blessed with a lovely daughter.

Seyi Tinubu Career

In a bid to change the narrative of advertising and improve the Nigerian economy positively, Seyi and his friend – Adenrele Olusoga, established Loatsad media. They got their license and started doing business full time.

Thus far, the company has grown from using static billboards to huge LEDs positioned in strategic locations. It has also become one of the biggest and most sought after advertising agency in Nigeria.

Seyi’s passion for nation building led him to establish the Noella Foundation – an umbrella foundation for the Seyi Tinubu Empowerment Programme (STEP).

Loatsad Promomedia

Loatsad Promomedia is an advertising company that places world-class billboards for its clients in Nigeria. The company emphasizes beauty and ambience. Also, they maximise consumer engagement through targeted messages at the right place and time. Loatsad Promomedia offers both outdoor advertising and digital advertising services to clients.

The outdoor advertising entails advertising for clients via lamp posts, flex banners, bridge panel, activation/events, inflatables, and LED boards. While digital advertising entails the use of geo-targeting technology, to reach the clients’ target audience, by placing clients ads at locations that are most important to the clients’ brand.

Loatsad promomedia creates online strategies, campaigns, and develop an overall online presence, that meets the clients’ overall digital need.

Noella Foundation

Seyi Tinubu established the Noella foundation in 2018, to improve the economic situation of Nigeria, via entrepreneurship, innovation, sustainable ideas, and action plans.

Noella Foundation is the umbrella body of the Seyi Tinubu Empowerment Project (STEP) aimed at creating a better Nigeria through job creation by supporting new tech startups with skills development and seed funding.

STEP provides business mentorship and networking opportunities for tech startups and gives them access to a community of like-minded pioneers in the tech space. In other words, the foundation focuses on nation building through human capital development.

Seyi Tinubu’s Volunteer Experience

Teld NGO

Seyi Tinubu is the co-founder and executive member of Teld NGO, founded in June 2005. The NGO was established to provide a better standard of living for youths and underprivileged children in Nigeria.

Teld achieves this feat by providing school fees and necessary supplies youths need to attend a boarding school and mentoring programs.

Bola Tinubu Foundation

Seyi Tinubu is the coordinator of the Bola Tinubu Foundation, founded in September 2012. The Bola Tinubu Foundation is an NGO with focus on education, poverty alleviation, and training.

Seyi organized, led, and coordinated the foundations’ outreach program in December 2013. During the outreach, 3,000 bags of rice and vegetable oils were distributed.

Youth Musical Fiesta

Seyi Tinubu is the coordinator of the Youth Musical Fiesta. Oluremi Tinubu started this program in June 2012. The program conveys youths of various churches and musical groups together. The musical fiesta is focused on children and youths between ages 7 – 18. The program holds once every year.
