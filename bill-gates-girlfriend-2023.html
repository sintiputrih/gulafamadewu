Yes, it is what you read. Paula Hurd was married to Mark Hurd, the CEO of the software giant Oracle and a former executive at Hewlett-Packard, before sparking romance rumors with Bill Gates. They were married for almost 30 years when he passed away in October 2019.

Paula Hurd is a graduate of the University of Texas

When we look at Paula Hurd’s educational background, we see that she earned a bachelor’s degree in business administration from the University of Texas in Austin in 1984.
