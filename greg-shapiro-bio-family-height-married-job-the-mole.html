Greg Shapiro Job

At the time of filming the show, Greg Shapiro was working as Marketing Consultant, he is currently working as Consumer Insights & Rewards Manager at City Experiences. He started his job in the company in June 2022. Before that, he was working at QualGym.

Greg started his career at Oxygen Media as a Digital / Editorial Intern in May 2009. After two months, he worked as a Production / Development Intern at Fox Cable Networks, MTV Networks, and @radical.media.

In 2012, Greg joined Film Sprout as a Project Coordinator where he only worked for seven months. He worked for nearly two and half years at Nickelodeon as a Research Coordinator.

Furthermore, Greg served Royal Caribbean Cruises Ltd. as Onboard Digital Content Specialist (Mar 2015 – Mar 2016), at Fathom Travel as Cultural Facilitator (Mar 2016 – Sep 2016), and as Research Analyst at Insight Strategy Group (Sep 2016 – Jan 2018).

From 2018 to 2020, Greg served Travelzoo as an account manager. He drove brand engagement, member acquisition, and booking conversions through integrated and multi-channel campaigns across email, mobile, cost-per-click, programmatic networks, Facebook and Instagram. While he was in the company, in 2019, grew account coverage from $300,000 to over $6 million in yearly revenue.

As the Cruise Vertical Lead, he also forecasted revenue for the Head of Production and develop strategic content proposals for the Head of Sales through PowerPoint storyboarding and soliciting bids from agencies and influencers. Moreover, he created and led pieces of training for onboarding new assistant and associate producers covering company culture, best practices for client and project management, and utilization of the many different back-office systems.

Greg is a Vassar College graduate with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Media Studies. Moreover, he is also a licensed Reiki practitioner and yoga and Zumba instructor.
