Thursday Night Football Overview

Thursday Night Football, a popular NFL series streaming on Prime Video, kicks off its 2023 season with the Kansas City Chiefs taking on the Detroit Lions on September 7, 2023, at 8:15 p.m. ET. The dynamic duo of Al Michaels and Kirk Herbstreit will provide commentary, while Kaylee Hartung reports from the sideline. Fans can catch the full schedule on the NFL’s website or Prime Video.

Known for intense competition, thrilling finishes, and unexpected comebacks, Thursday Night Football has delivered memorable moments, reigniting rivalries, showcasing star players, and surprising audiences with underdog victories.

The viewing experience for Thursday Night Football has evolved as games are now available not only on traditional TV networks but also on streaming platforms like Amazon Prime Video, making it convenient for fans to tune in from anywhere.

Today’s Chiefs-Lions Game Announcers on NBC

For the Chiefs-Lions game airing on NBC today, viewers can enjoy commentary from Mike Tirico, who will handle the play-by-play, and Cris Collinsworth, providing color analysis. Melissa Stark will report from the sidelines. The game marks the start of the 2023 NFL regular season and is eagerly anticipated by football enthusiasts. Kickoff is set for 8:20 p.m. ET.

NFL Network’s Coverage

The NFL Network is gearing up for the 2023 season with extensive coverage, beginning with the annual NFL Kickoff Game featuring the Kansas City Chiefs and the Detroit Lions. NBC plans to broadcast 18 regular season Sunday Night Football games, including special events like the NFL Kickoff Game and a Thanksgiving primetime matchup. Additionally, Peacock, the streaming service, will showcase an exclusive Week 16 game between the Buffalo Bills and the Los Angeles Chargers.

During the postseason, NBC and Peacock will bring fans two games during Super Wild Card Weekend and one game in the divisional round, offering football enthusiasts a thrilling conclusion to the 2023 NFL season.

Watching Thursday Night Football on Amazon Prime

To stream Thursday Night Football on Amazon Prime Video, ensure you have an active Amazon Prime membership. These games typically air live on Thursday nights throughout the NFL season. Simply access the Prime Video app on your device, log in with your Prime credentials, and enjoy watching the games.
